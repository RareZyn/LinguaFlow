RULE 1: Symbolic Expressions

  5 + 3                     → 8
  10 - 4                    → 6
  6 * 7                     → 42
  15 / 3                    → 5
  10 / 4                    → 2.5
  (5 + 3) * 2               → 16
  ((2 + 3) * (4 - 1))       → 15
  2 + 3 * 4                 → 14  (precedence: multiply first)
  10 - 6 / 2                → 7   (precedence: divide first)
  -5                        → -5  (unary minus)
  10 + -3                   → 7
  --5                       → 5   (double negation)
  3.5 + 2.5                 → 6.0
  2 + 3 * 4 - 6 / 2         → 11

  ---
  RULE 2: Infix Worded Expressions

  5 add 3                   → 8
  10 subtract 4             → 6
  6 multiply 7              → 42
  15 divide 3               → 5
  7 plus 8                  → 15
  20 minus 5                → 15
  4 times 5                 → 20
  10 add 5 subtract 3       → 12  (chained)
  5.5 add 4.5               → 10.0
  2 add 3 times 4           → 14  (precedence preserved)

  ---
  RULE 3: Functional Form (List Operations)

  sum these numbers: [5, 3, 7]           → 15
  multiply these numbers: [2, 3, 4]      → 24
  subtract these numbers: [20, 5, 3]     → 12  (left-to-right: 20-5-3)
  divide these numbers: [100, 5, 2]      → 10  (left-to-right: 100/5/2)
  sum these numbers: [1.5, 2.5, 3.0]     → 7.0
  sum these numbers: [42]                → 42  (single element)
  add these numbers: [10, 20, 30]        → 60
  product these numbers: [2, 2, 2]       → 8
  total these numbers: [1, 2, 3, 4]      → 10

  ---
  RULE 4: Natural Phrasing

  sum of 5 and 3                → 8
  product of 10 and 2.5         → 25
  difference of 20 and 5        → 15
  quotient of 15 and 3          → 5
  sum of 3.5 and 2.5            → 6.0
  add of 10 and 5               → 15
  multiply of 6 and 7           → 42

  ---
  RULE 5: Mixed Rules (Symbolic + Worded)

  5 + 4 subtract 2              → 7   (Rule 1 + Rule 2)
  10 * 2 add 5                  → 25  (Rule 1 + Rule 2)

  ---
  RULE 6: Natural Language Conversion

  calc what is 10 divided by 2        → 5
  calc what is 5 plus 3               → 8
  calc calculate 5 times 3            → 15

  ---
  RULE 7: Variable Assignment

  create x as 5                 → 5
  x                             → 5
  create result as 10 + 5       → 15
  result                        → 15
  create a as 10
  create b as 20
  a + b                         → 30
  create x as 5
  create y as 3
  sum of x and y                → 8

  ---
  RULE 8: Function Definition & Call

  create double taking x do
  x * 2
  end
  find double 5                 → 10

  create add taking a b do
  a + b
  end
  find add 3 7                  → 10

  create calc taking a b c do
  a + b * c
  end
  find calc 1 2 3               → 7  (1 + 2*3)

  ---
  ERROR HANDLING

  10 / 0                        → Runtime Error: Division by zero
  5 @ 3                         → Illegal Character: '@'
  undefined_var + 5             → Runtime Error: 'undefined_var' is not defined      
  divide these numbers: [10, 0] → Runtime Error: Division by zero

  ---
  LLM SYNONYM RESOLUTION

  5 accumulate 3                → 8   (LLM resolves to +)
  10 combine 5                  → 15  (LLM resolves to +)
  total these numbers: [1, 2, 3, 4]    → 10
  aggregate these numbers: [5, 10, 15] → 30

  ---
  EDGE CASES

  0 + 5                         → 5
  5 * 0                         → 0
  -5 + 10                       → 5
  -5 * -3                       → 15
  1000000 + 2000000             → 3000000
  0.001 + 0.002                 → 0.003
  42                            → 42  (single number)
  (((1 + 2)))                   → 3   (nested parentheses)
  1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5             → 15
  10 - 3 + 5 - 2 + 1            → 11
